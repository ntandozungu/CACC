Our Vision

Spreading the Gospel, Building Community, Transforming Lives

At Christ Ambassadors Community Church, we are called to be ambassadors of Christ in our community and beyond. We believe in the transformative power of God's love and are committed to sharing His message of hope, redemption, and eternal life with all who seek Him.

Faith

Grounded in Scripture, growing in our relationship with Christ

Community

Building meaningful relationships and supporting one another

Service

Serving others with love, compassion, and humility

Outreach

Reaching the world with the Gospel of Jesus Christ
